**blueprint/03-game-design-bible.md** (BLUE-03):
Write the Game Design Bible -- the mechanical backbone of the entire game. This is the most critical blueprint section.

Sections to include:

**Core Loop (minute-to-minute):**
- Trigger: prayer time or habit reminder
- Action: open app, tap to complete habit
- Reward: XP gain animation, streak increment, progress bar movement
- Investment: streak length increases, XP bar fills toward next level
- Loop duration: 5-30 seconds per completion

**Meta Loop (day-to-day):**
- Daily quest check-in (what quests are active, progress)
- Habit completions throughout the day (morning Fajr, midday Dhuhr, etc.)
- Evening Muhasabah reflection (30-60 seconds)
- Daily XP total review
- Loop duration: 3-10 minutes total daily engagement

**Long Loop (week-to-month):**
- Weekly quest rotation and completion
- Title unlock milestones approaching
- HUD world evolution at level thresholds
- Streak milestone celebrations (7, 14, 30, 40, 100 days)
- Monthly consistency review

**XP Economy Model:**
Define the exact formula: `XP_required(level) = base_xp * (level ^ exponent)`
- Tune constants so: Level 5 by day 3, Level 8 by day 7, Level 20 by month 1, Level 50 by month 6, Level 100 by year 1+
- Base XP per habit completion: define values by habit type (salah: 15 XP, Quran reading: 20 XP, dhikr: 10 XP, fasting: 25 XP, custom: 15 XP)
- Streak multiplier: 1.0x base, +0.1x per consecutive day on that habit, capped at 3.0x (so day 21+ = 3.0x)
- Quest bonus XP: daily quests 25-50 XP, weekly quests 100-200 XP, stretch quests 300-500 XP
- Muhasabah completion bonus: 10 XP (small, no pressure to do it)

**XP Simulation Table:**
Create a full table for levels 1-100 with columns: Level | XP to Next | Cumulative XP | Est. Days (Consistent Player: 5 habits/day) | Unlock
Include milestone rows at minimum: 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50, 60, 75, 100.
Run three player archetype simulations:
- Casual (2 habits/day, no quests): show level at day 7, 30, 90, 365
- Consistent (5 habits/day, daily quests): show level at day 7, 30, 90, 365
- Power (8 habits/day, all quests): show level at day 7, 30, 90, 365

**Streak Model:**
- Per-habit streaks (each habit has its own streak counter)
- Overall discipline streak (did user complete ANY habit today? yes = streak continues)
- Streak multiplier formula (per-habit, not global)
- Streak break: multiplier resets to 1.0x, XP total NEVER decreases
- Streak milestones: 7, 14, 21, 30, 40, 60, 90, 100, 365 days -- each with a celebration moment

**Failure and Recovery (Mercy Mode):**
- Trigger: any habit streak breaks (0 completions for that habit in a day)
- Response: NO shame notification. Next app open shows Mercy Mode banner with compassionate message + recovery quest
- Recovery quest examples: "Complete [habit] 3 times in the next 3 days to rebuild momentum"
- Recovery reward: partial streak credit (e.g., streak shows "Rebuilt: 3 days" not "0 days")
- Mercy Mode copy tone: blend of brief Islamic wisdom ("The door of tawbah is always open") + game action ("Recovery quest unlocked")

**Anti-Burnout Mechanisms:**
- Daily XP cap: soft cap at ~500 XP/day (diminishing returns after, not hard cutoff)
- No punishment for days off (XP never decreases, no negative notifications)
- Quest variety prevents repetitive grind
- Muhasabah is always optional (10 XP bonus is gentle nudge, not required)
- Weekly summary focuses on "days active this week" not "days missed"

**Boss Progression (v2 reference):**
One-paragraph summary of Nafs Boss Arena and Dopamine Detox Dungeon with placeholder XP values. Mark clearly as "v2 -- included for economy coherence only."

**Friday Power-Ups (v2 reference):**
One-paragraph summary of Jumu'ah bonuses. Mark as v2.

Add cross-reference links to sections 01, 02, 04, and 05.
