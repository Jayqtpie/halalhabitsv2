Local `playerStats` used for `TitleGrid` progress bars hardcodes `mercyRecoveries: 0` and `muhasabahStreak: 0`. Actual title unlock logic in `gameStore.checkTitles` correctly queries real DB data. Display-only gap — users see 0/N progress on mercy/muhasabah title bars even after real progress. (Severity: Low | Affects: GAME-03 display)
